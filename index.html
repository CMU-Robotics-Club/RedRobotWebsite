Our Mission

Red Robot Hackathon aims to be an unforgettable experience for novice and veteran hackers alike!

We (the organizers) are a group of passionate hackers and makers from the CMU Robotics Club who want to share our love of hardware hacking with as many people as possible. We realized that hardware hacking usually has a pretty high barrier to entry -- once you have an idea, you need a pretty expansive collection of parts, tools, and expertise to bring your ideas to life. Red Robot Hackathon is our effort to lower this barrier as much as possible. We want you to be able to bring your ideas to life regardless of your previous experience or expertise. Each team will receive a starter kit of parts, which can either be used for free form hacking, or to participate in our secret competition. Mentorship is available for everyone, from those just getting started with hardware hacking to those pushing the boundaries of what's possible. To fuel the whole event, we'll have plenty of food, drinks, and prizes available. We can't wait to see what you make!

Why Red Robot?

Why should you spend your weekend at Red Robot Hackathon?

Beginner Friendly

We'll have instructions on how to build a robot from the starter kit, which you can expand on and use to participate in our secret competition!

Mentorship

Mentors will be available all weekend to help with anything and everything related to hardware hacking!

Networking

Meet new people who share your interests, hang out with your team, and make friends with all of our generous sponsors!

Prizes

No hackathon would be complete without prizes. We'll have awards for both freeform hacking and our secret competition!

Inspiration

Bring the project you've always dreamed of to life, and be inspired by those around you doing the same thing!

Swag

You'll get to keep the projects you make (including the starter kits), and get a Red Robot Hackathon t-shirt too!
